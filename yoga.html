5 Yogas to Practice

1. Tadasana (Mountain Pose)

Step 1: Stand straight with feet together.
Step 2: Keep arms at sides, palms open.
Step 3: Take a deep breath and raise arms overhead.
Step 4: Hold position for 30 sec to 1 min.
Step 5: Exhale and return to original position.

2. Vrikshasana (Tree Pose)

Step 1: Stand straight, shift weight to left leg.
Step 2: Place right foot on inner left thigh.
Step 3: Join palms in prayer at chest.
Step 4: Hold balance for 30 sec.
Step 5: Switch legs and repeat.

3. Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Step 1: Lie on stomach, hands under shoulders.
Step 2: Inhale and lift chest using back muscles.
Step 3: Keep elbows close to body.
Step 4: Hold for 15‚Äì30 sec.
Step 5: Exhale and relax.

4. Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward Dog)

Step 1: Start on hands and knees.
Step 2: Lift hips up to form inverted V.
Step 3: Keep hands and feet grounded.
Step 4: Hold for 30 sec.
Step 5: Breathe deeply and release.

5. Shavasana (Corpse Pose)

Step 1: Lie flat on back, arms at sides.
Step 2: Close eyes, relax body.
Step 3: Breathe naturally.
Step 4: Hold for 5‚Äì10 min.
Step 5: Slowly sit up.
